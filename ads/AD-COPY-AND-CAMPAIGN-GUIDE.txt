=====================================================
  FACEBOOK/INSTAGRAM TARGETING RECOMMENDATIONS
=====================================================

CAMPAIGN 1: AWARENESS
  Objective: Reach / Brand Awareness
  Budget: $10-15/day
  Duration: Start NOW, run through March 22

  Targeting:
  - Location: 25-mile radius around Charleston, SC
  - Age: 25-45
  - Gender: All (but women will likely convert more)
  - Interests:
    * Parenting
    * Motherhood
    * Easter
    * Family activities
    * Kids activities
    * Charleston SC community groups
  - Behaviors:
    * Parents with children ages 0-12

  Placements: Facebook Feed, Instagram Feed, Instagram Stories

  Ads to use: Ad #1 (Awareness), Ad #3 (Story)


CAMPAIGN 2: REGISTRATION / CONVERSION
  Objective: Conversions (or Traffic if pixel not set up)
  Budget: $15-25/day
  Duration: Start 2 weeks after awareness, run through March 28

  Targeting: Same as above, PLUS:
  - Custom Audience: Retarget website visitors
  - Lookalike Audience: Based on form submissions

  Placements: Facebook Feed, Instagram Feed, Instagram Stories

  Ads to use: Ad #2 (Registration), Ad #4 (Carousel)


CAMPAIGN 3: URGENCY / LAST CALL
  Objective: Conversions
  Budget: $20-30/day
  Duration: March 15 — April 2 (last push)

  Targeting:
  - Retarget: People who visited site but didn't register
  - Same interest targeting as above

  Ads to use: Ad #5 (Testimonial), Ad #6 (Urgency)


=====================================================
  TOTAL SUGGESTED AD BUDGET
=====================================================

  Awareness (4 weeks):    $10/day x 28 = $280
  Registration (3 weeks): $20/day x 21 = $420
  Urgency (2.5 weeks):    $25/day x 18 = $450

  TOTAL: ~$1,150 for the full campaign

  MINIMUM viable budget: $500-600
  (Run awareness + registration only, $10-15/day)
